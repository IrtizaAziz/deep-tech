## 1. The Selected IP

### 1.1 IP Descriptions

**IP 1 — AI-Enhanced Secure Medical Records Management** (PI 2025007955, TRL 5, MR 5)

A computer-implemented system enabling healthcare providers and digital health platforms to **upload, store, share, and intelligently query medical records** under strict security conditions. Core technical capabilities include:

- Encrypted cloud storage with audit trails
- OCR-based digitisation of scanned medical documents
- Role-based access control (RBAC) with granular permissions
- Time-limited, permission-controlled record sharing via secure access links
- A secure viewer with copy/forward restrictions
- AI-powered record summarisation for faster clinical review
- An intelligent chatbot interface for contextual, query-based record navigation
- An emergency access override mode for urgent clinical scenarios

The system is designed for **cloud-native, enterprise-grade deployment** across hospitals, clinics, telemedicine platforms, health information exchanges, insurance processors, and corporate healthcare providers.

**IP 2 — Interactive Supplement Intake Assessment Method** (UI 2025002953, TRL 3, MR 2)

A computer-implemented assessment framework that captures a patient's supplement intake patterns through a **structured survey instrument**, then processes responses through a **dual-engine architecture**:

1. **AI Algorithm** — trained on expert-verified, pre-labelled medical data to detect risky supplement combinations and flag potential drug-supplement interactions
2. **Statistical Scoring Model** — evaluates patient variables (dosage, frequency, health profile, comorbidities) to generate risk/benefit scores

The system delivers **real-time, personalised recommendations** to both patients and clinicians, enabling informed self-care and evidence-based clinical intervention.

### 1.2 Combined Product Vision

**Healynx** merges these two IPs into a single platform where:

- Patients complete a structured supplement/lifestyle intake survey via mobile or web
- The Supplement Intelligence Engine processes responses using the dual AI + statistical model
- Results are securely stored, summarised, and surfaced within the patient's encrypted medical record
- Healthcare providers access AI-generated summaries, interaction alerts, and preventive health insights through a unified clinical dashboard
- Records can be shared securely on a time-limited basis with specialists, insurers, or telemedicine providers

The integration creates a **closed-loop preventive healthcare system**: intake data flows into secure records, which feed AI-driven insights, which inform clinical decisions, which feed back into personalised patient recommendations.
