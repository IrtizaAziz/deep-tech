### SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM *(~45 sec)*

Mr. Rajan is 58. Diabetes. Hypertension. Three prescriptions from his doctor — and four supplements he's taking on his own. Fish oil. Fenugreek. Bitter melon. Jamu.

His doctor knows almost nothing about those four.

One of them may be potentiating his Metformin — silently driving his blood sugar to dangerous lows. His doctor, seeing 40 patients that day, has no systematic way to know.

This is a system failure. It affects 60% of GP caseloads. Records are fragmented across paper files and clinic systems. Supplements go undisclosed. And 15 to 20% of every consultation is wasted gathering data that should already be there.

Mr. Rajan is not an edge case. He is the majority.

*→ And this problem isn't shrinking — it's accelerating.*

The first platform in Malaysia combining three things that have never existed together — AI-driven supplement intelligence, secure medical records, and preventive health analytics.

### SLIDE 5 — THE CLOSED LOOP *(~35 sec)*

Mr. Rajan completes a three-minute supplement survey before his appointment. Our dual-engine AI processes it in under three seconds — cross-referencing every supplement against his medications using our proprietary Malaysian clinical dataset.

Before he even sits down, his doctor's dashboard flags a high-severity alert: fenugreek may potentiate Metformin — hypoglycemia risk.

Everything is encrypted in Malaysian AWS cloud with immutable audit trails. The doctor acts. Mr. Rajan leaves safer than he has ever been.

*→ Mr. Rajan is one patient. The market is millions.*
