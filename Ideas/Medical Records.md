✓** TECHNOLOGY OVERVIEW**

This technology is a **secure, AI-enhanced digital medical records management** **system** designed to help healthcare providers, platforms, and digital health companies **store, share, and use medical records safely and efficiently**. 

The system combines **strong data security**, **controlled access management**, and **artificial intelligence** to support modern healthcare workflows. It allows medical records to be uploaded, securely stored, shared on a time-limited basis, and intelligently summarized, improving accessibility while maintaining strict data protection standards. 

The solution is designed for **cloud-based deployment** and integration into existing healthcare or health-tech platforms. 

✓** FEATURES AND SPECIFICATIONS**

•

Encrypted cloud storage for secure medical record management

•

Mobile and web-based interfaces for record upload and access

•

Optical character recognition \(OCR\) for digitising scanned medical documents

•

Role-based access control to manage viewing, sharing, and usage permissions

•

Time-limited, permission-controlled record sharing via secure access links

•

Secure viewer with view-only restrictions to prevent unauthorised copying

•

AI-powered record summarisation for faster clinical or administrative review





****• Intelligent chatbot interface for contextual, query-based record navigation

•

Emergency access mode to allow temporary authorised access when needed
