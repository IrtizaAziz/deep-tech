## Instructions for the Designer / AI

Create a 12-slide pitch deck for **Healynx** — an AI-powered preventive healthcare platform built on two Universiti Malaya patents. The deck must tell a story that moves from villain (the problem) to weapon (the solution) to conquest (the market and ask). Every number, name, and claim must come from the data below — do not invent statistics.

## Slide 2 — The Pain (30 seconds)

**Goal:** Make the problem visceral. Lead with a real persona to humanise the pain before showing statistics.

**Left side:** An illustration or silhouette of a doctor at a desk — surrounded by paper records, phone-camera scans, and scattered pill bottles. Use a muted, slightly dark palette to signal chaos and stress.

**Right side:** Introduce the persona and list three pain points.

**Persona:** Dr. Aminah — General Practitioner, private clinic, Klang Valley. Sees 40–50 patients per day. ~60% of her caseload have at least one chronic condition.

**Pain 1 (icon: block/ban):** Patients routinely fail to disclose supplement, herbal, and traditional remedy use during 10-minute consultations — creating hidden drug-interaction blind spots.

**Pain 2 (icon: document):** Medical records are fragmented across clinics, paper files, and phone-camera scans. Not searchable. No AI to extract clinical meaning.

**Pain 3 (icon: warning/scale):** No automated, localised way to cross-check drug–supplement interactions at point of care — creating medico-legal liability for every undocumented supplement use.

**Footer quote (italic, muted):** "Opening a consultation without knowing the patient's jamu prep can react with their antihypertensive — that risk is invisible today."

**Secondary persona for spoken delivery:** Mr. Rajan — 58 years old, Type 2 diabetes and hypertension. Takes 3 prescription medications (metformin, amlodipine, atorvastatin) and 4 supplements including garlic extract and a traditional jamu preparation. Records scattered across two clinics and printed lab reports. Worried about interactions but no one systematically checks.

**Speaker note:** "Imagine Dr. Aminah — a GP in a busy Klang Valley clinic. She chose medicine to heal. But every day she's trapped in a labyrinth of invisible supplement risks and fragmented records she can't search. Meanwhile her patient Mr. Rajan is silently taking a jamu preparation that could amplify his blood pressure medication. Nobody knows."

## Slide 6 — How It Works (25 seconds)

**Goal:** Simplify the architecture into three steps. Make it legible in one glance. No one funds what they don't understand.

**Section label:** "HOW HEALYNX WORKS"

**Three-step horizontal flow with connecting arrows:**

**Step 01 — CAPTURE (📋 icon):**
Patient completes a structured supplement intake survey on mobile or web — in under 3 minutes. The survey captures supplement name, dosage, frequency, duration, health condition, existing medications, allergies, and comorbidities. Pre-populated from last completed survey for returning patients. Renders from 320px mobile to 1920px desktop.

**Step 02 — ANALYSE (🤖 icon):**
Dual-engine AI processes the survey responses using IP 2 (UI 2025002953): an AI algorithm trained on expert-verified Malaysian medical data detects drug-supplement and supplement-supplement interactions, while a statistical scoring model evaluates patient variables to produce a numeric risk score (0–100). Results returned in under 3 seconds. Target sensitivity: ≥85% against clinical pharmacist review.

**Step 03 — ACT (🔒 icon):**
Results land simultaneously in the patient's AES-256 encrypted medical record (IP 1 — PI 2025007955) and the doctor's clinical dashboard. Patient receives a wellness score and plain-language recommendations. Clinician receives severity-coded alert cards within 10 seconds. Emergency access override is available 24/7 with automatic audit logging.

**Footer:** "Powered by IP UI 2025002953 (Supplement AI) + PI 2025007955 (Secure Records)"
