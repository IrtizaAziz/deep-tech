✓** TECHNOLOGY OVERVIEW**

This invention provides a **computer-implemented method** for assessing a patient’s supplement intake using **real-time AI-driven analysis**. Data from a structured **survey instrument** is processed by an integrated **expert-validated** **algorithm** and **statistical model**, generating **personalized recommendations** for both patients and healthcare providers. 

By combining **expert-labelled medical data** with predictive analytics, the system empowers **informed clinical decision-making**, reduces errors in supplement use, and enhances patient care outcomes. 

✓** FEATURES AND SPECIFICATIONS**

**Survey-Based Data Collection:** Captures supplement intake patterns, dosage, frequency, and related health variables. 

**Dual Algorithm Integration**

●

**AI Algorithm** trained on expert-verified, pre-labelled medical data. 

●

**Statistical Scoring Model** evaluating patient variables for risk and benefit assessment. 

**Real-Time Analysis:** Immediate processing and feedback upon data submission. 

**Personalized Output: **Tailored insights and recommendations for both patient self-management and clinical interventions. 





**Scalable Platform:** Adaptable for use in clinics, telehealth systems, and research studies. 

✓** POTENTIAL APPLICATION**

**Clinical Settings:** Support healthcare professionals in managing supplement use among patients. 

**Preventive Healthcare:** Identify potential supplement-drug interactions or nutrient deficiencies. 

**Telemedicine & Digital Health:** Integration with patient portals and mobile health applications. 

**Research & Public Health:** Large-scale data collection for population health studies on supplement usage trends. 

✓** UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITIONS**

**Immediate Feedback:** Supports real-time clinical decision-making.

**Integration Capability:** Compatible with EMR/EHR systems for streamlined workflow.
